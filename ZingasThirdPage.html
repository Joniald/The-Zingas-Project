The beginning...

Hi! My name is Athanasia and I am a girl in my 30s (almost!). I was born in Athens, the beautiful capital city of Greece, where I still live. I am a handcrafter and artist in the soul. I love being in the nature and that doesnâ€™t make it a surprise that my favourite colors are blue for the sky, brown for earth, green for trees and red for strawberries. Yummy!!

I also have a dog best friend. His name is Ginga but in time it changed to Zinga, as many started calling him! Zinga himself was the reason for his name to be changed (ultra good dogs get a lot of calls! A LOT!) and for me, my original wills to be revealed. I realized I wanted to create things that people love. A smile on a face, a wagging tail, a big old healthy tree etc. is what makes world beautiful to me and inspires me! However wasn't sure if that could be a chair that transforms to a table or a dress for another girl out there! After experimenting on various staff and as time was passing by I decided that jewelry making was my thing!

As a result, now, I am a self-taught macrame jewelry designer and after studying metalsmithing for 2 years I decided to expand my creativity. Every piece in my shop is handmade with extra love and positive energy! I use whatever material comes my way to inspire me, semi-precious stones, beads, metal, shapes, colors... Hope you like my work and stay tuned cause there is more to come!!

The Zingas Project is a unique macrame line of the handmade jewellery created by Athanasia Vlasopoulou, a passionate Greek designer.
